Artificial Intelligence (AI) holds immense potential to revolutionize industries across the globe. However, as we move towards an increasingly AI-driven world, it is crucial to consider the ethical implications of this technological advancement.

Why we need AI

One major reason humans need AI is because it allows us to harness the collective intelligence of billions of people. AI learns from large amounts of data, which enables it to adapt to new situations and improve over time. Additionally, AI can perform complex tasks much faster than humans, freeing up valuable time for people to focus on other areas. AI can also help enhance human capabilities, such as augmenting our memory or even aiding in decision-making processes. Ultimately, AI plays a crucial role in shaping the future of technology and the global economy, positioning humans to become more innovative and productive in the face of unprecedented challenges
